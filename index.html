About me

Welcome You!!!

Samsuddin Ahmed

Gazipur, Dhaka, Bangladesh

I am Mr. Samsuddin to a colleague and to an acquaintance, Khokon to family members and close friends. Research interest includes Security,Robotics Motion and Manipulation.Sensing Technologies, Data Science, Computer Vision, Health Data Processing, Medical Image Analysis and Diseases Diagnosis, Information Security, Machine Learning, etc. Born and grew up in an edu-oriented respectful family in the southeastern part of Bangladesh where culturally diverse ethnicities live in harmony. I am educated at the University of Chittagong, Bangladesh, and then at Chosun University, Korea. I have been teaching at the University level since 2010 and now working as an Assistant Professor and Chairman of the Department of IoT and Robotics Engineering (Former Information and Communication Technology) at Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Digital University, Bangladesh. From March 2018 to August 2021, I was a research assistant at the Computer Vision Lab of Chosun University, South Korea, under the supervision of Professor Ho Yub Jung. I continuously contribute to the research community in addition to my teaching at the university.
